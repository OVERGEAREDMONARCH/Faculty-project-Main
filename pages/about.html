About Bridges Journal

About the Journal

Bridges: Integrative Journal of Natural and Applied Sciences is a peer-reviewed publication that covers various fields including biology, chemistry, physics, engineering, and environmental sciences. Our mission is to encourage interdisciplinary collaboration and share innovative research worldwide.

This journal is under the Faculty of Natural and Applied Sciences at Lead City University, striving to promote academic excellence and self-reliance.

Our Mission

Bridges: Integrative Journal of Natural and Applied Sciences serves as a premier platform for interdisciplinary research in the natural and applied sciences. Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical frameworks and practical applications, fostering collaboration across scientific disciplines.

Journal Scope

We publish high-quality research papers, reviews, and short communications in:

  • Physical Sciences
  • Biological Sciences
  • Environmental Sciences
  • Mathematics and Statistics
  • Computer Science and Technology
  • Engineering Sciences

Publication Process

Our rigorous peer-review process ensures the highest standards of scientific integrity:

  1. Initial submission screening
  2. Double-blind peer review
  3. Editorial decision
  4. Author revision (if required)
  5. Final acceptance and publication

Open Access Policy

Bridges Journal is committed to making research freely available to the global scientific community. We operate on an open-access model, ensuring that all published articles are immediately accessible to readers worldwide without subscription barriers.
