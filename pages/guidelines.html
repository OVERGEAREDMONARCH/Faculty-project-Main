Author Guidelines

Preparing Your Manuscript

When submitting a manuscript to Bridges: Integrative Journal of Science and Technology, please ensure it adheres to the following guidelines:

  • Language: Manuscripts must be written in clear, concise English.
  • Format: Use a standard font (e.g., Times New Roman), 12 pt size, double-spaced.
  • Structure: Include Title, Abstract, Keywords, Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion, Conclusion, and References.
  • References: Follow APA or Harvard referencing style (check with the editorial board for consistent format).
  • Figures and Tables: Number all figures and tables consecutively; include captions and cite them in the text.

Submission Checklist

Prior to submission, ensure that:

  • All authors have approved the final manuscript.
  • The paper has not been published elsewhere.
  • The manuscript includes complete references and citations.
  • Ethical guidelines are followed (especially for studies involving human or animal subjects).
