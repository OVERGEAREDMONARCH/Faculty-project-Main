Board Members

Reviewers will be added and updated regularly. Manuscript editors are encouraged to suggest reviewers for their respective sections. All Professors and Heads of Departments (HODs) within the faculty are members of the board.

Editorial Policies

Our board upholds rigorous ethical guidelines and ensures all manuscripts adhere to international standards of scholarly publication. We operate a double-blind peer review process, safeguarding fairness and transparency.
