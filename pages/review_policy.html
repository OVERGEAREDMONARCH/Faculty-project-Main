Review Policy

Peer-Review Process

Bridges: Integrative Journal of Natural and Applied Sciences follows a rigorous double-blind peer-review process. The identities of both reviewers and authors are kept anonymous to ensure unbiased feedback.

  • Each submission is first evaluated by the Editor-in-Chief or Associate Editor for suitability.
  • Qualified manuscripts are then sent to at least two independent reviewers with expertise in the relevant field.
  • Reviewers evaluate the manuscript based on originality, methodology, significance, clarity, and ethical standards.

Ethical Considerations

Bridges maintains strict policies against plagiarism, data falsification, and other ethical misconduct. Any manuscript found in violation may be rejected or retracted.
