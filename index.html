About the Journal

Bridges: Integrative Journal of Science and Technology publishes original research articles, reviews, and short communications spanning all areas of science and technology. As a journal under the Faculty of Natural and Applied Sciences at Lead City University, we aim to provide a global platform for scholarly discourse, fostering innovation and interdisciplinary collaboration.
